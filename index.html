FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What Is DuSeat And How Does It Work?

DuSeat is a platform that connects investors with verified real estate agents in Dubai. You can explore opportunities, chat with agents, and invest remotely.

Who Can Use DuSeat?

Anyone interested in property investment can use DuSeat — from first-time buyers to experienced investors looking for premium opportunities.

Are The Agents Verified?

Yes, all agents on DuSeat are verified and licensed professionals registered with trusted real estate authorities across the UAE.

Can I Invest Remotely?

Absolutely! DuSeat allows you to complete your investment process online from anywhere in the world with full transparency.

How Do I Track My Investment?

You can monitor every stage of your investment through your DuSeat dashboard and receive real-time updates directly from your agent.

Is There Any Service Fee?

DuSeat is completely free for investors. Agents may pay a small commission after a successful property transaction.
